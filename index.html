Our Mission

Our longstanding history of delivering amazing salmon cookies to the world has witheld through tough times. We aim to bring joy to the world by not only making our cookies edible for human consumption but also for aquatic life. Our product is ocean-friendly!

For every cookie you purchase, we match half of the price per cookie to the Marine Life Foundation(MLF). MLF strives to create a cleaner environment for sea creatures. So far, the foundation has rid the ocean of over 15 tons of plastic debris that lingers in the wild waters all over the world!
